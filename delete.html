App name: Smart Highlighter: AI Reader

Developer: Innovation Generation

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Delete data on your device (mobile app)

  1. Open the Smart Highlighter app on your Android or iOS device.
  2. Tap the Privacy icon in the top app bar.
  3. Open Privacy & Data.
  4. Tap Delete All Local Data and confirm.

This permanently removes from your device:

  • OCR text and AI highlights
  • Bookmarks and study notes
  • Reading progress and app preferences stored locally

Request deletion of server-processed data

If you used AI features (summaries, quizzes, flashcards) or cloud OCR, text or images may have been sent to our server for processing. To request deletion of backend data, email:

support@innovationgeneration.com

Include the subject line: Data Deletion Request - Smart Highlighter

Data deleted vs retained

  • Deleted immediately: local app data when you use Delete All Local Data
  • Deleted on request: backend processing data (within 30 days of verified email)
  • May retain: support emails for legal/security; anonymous logs without personal content
