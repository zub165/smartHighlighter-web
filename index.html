🔧 Camera Troubleshooting

1. Check Browser Permissions:

Click the camera icon in your browser's address bar and ensure camera access is allowed.

2. Use HTTPS or Localhost:

Camera access requires a secure connection. Use HTTPS or localhost (like now).

3. Close Other Camera Apps:

Make sure no other applications are using your camera.

4. Check Camera Hardware:

Ensure your camera is properly connected and not being used by other software.

5. Try Different Browser:

Some browsers have better camera support than others.

📱 Mobile-Specific Tips:

iPhone: Use Safari, allow camera permissions in Settings > Safari > Camera

Android: Use Chrome, allow camera permissions when prompted

Both: Make sure no other apps are using the camera
