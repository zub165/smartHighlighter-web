Smart Highlighter Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 5, 2026

Overview

Smart Highlighter helps users scan book pages, extract text with OCR, and generate AI-powered study highlights. This policy applies to the web app, Android app, and iOS app.

Data We Collect

  • Camera and photo library: Used only to capture or import pages for OCR. Images are processed on-device when possible.
  • Extracted text: Stored locally on your device (bookmarks, OCR text, reading progress).
  • AI requests: When you request summaries, quizzes, or flashcards, extracted text is sent to our backend for processing.

How We Use Data

  • Provide OCR and reading assistance features
  • Generate AI summaries and study tools
  • Improve reliability and support

Data Storage

Local data is stored on your device. Backend processing is used for AI features and optional cloud OCR fallback.

Data Deletion

Delete local data in the app: Privacy icon → Privacy & Data → Delete All Local Data. See delete.html for full steps. For backend deletion, email support@innovationgeneration.com.

Third Parties

We may use OpenAI and OCR libraries to process text you submit. We do not sell personal data.

Contact
